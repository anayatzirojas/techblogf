TECH INTERVIEW

By: Anayatzi Rojas

I interviewed my mother. She was born on July 9, 1974 and she is 44 years old.

  • First Name: Maria
  • Last Name:Mosso

Ana: How old were you when you got your first computer?

Maria: I got my first computer when I was 16 years old because my cousin moved and she ended up giving the computer to my family.

Ana: What was your favorite piece of tech?

Maria: It was probably the big TV that we had just becasue it was the only thing we really had as a kid.

Ana: How has tech changed over your lifetime?

Maria: The tech has gotten so much more advanced and people are getting much more smater and that allows us to keep advancing in the tech world. We went from the big block phone to the small iPhone x that fit into our pockets.

Ana: Has the tech hurt of helped our society?

Maria: The tech that we have now has hurt our world so much because kids grow up with everything getting handed to them like phones, computers, tablets and games and they dont work for the things and end up not paying attention to the real world.

Ana: What is your greatest hope for tech in the future?

Maria: The greatest hope I have for tech in the future would be to get more advanced in medicane field.

Ana:What is your greatest fear for tech in the future?

Maria: The fear would be losing jobs because of the robots

Ana: What advice would you like to share with the younger generation about tech?

Maria: Just not to be focused on the tech but mainly on your studies.
